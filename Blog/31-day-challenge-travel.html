31 Day Challenge - Travel

Dec 02, 2021

I want to travel from one edge of the known world to the other. How do I go about this, and is it possible?

Cutting through the clouds of the heavens are the large Aeronavis ships.  Designed for war, repurposed for luxury.  They move the wealthy across the world like massive exotic flying yachts.  While not the fastest they are effective and provide an experience and relaxation many of the wealthy desire.

Marbled ballrooms, five-course grand meals, enclosed herb gardens, magical swimming pools, and more are all accessible in these ships.  While most of the people on them are Wizards they are more focused on the experience of the travel.

In a world where people can be teleported around with ease why fly?  Teleporting can cause some to feel sick or suffer side effects.  Only those who are trained in Teleporting can experience can do so with no side effects.  This is when even a high magic place like the [[Atheneum]] can have use for Aeronavis, brooms, and boats.
