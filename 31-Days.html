31 Days

Jax's homebrew world has been played in for over 8 years.  This 31 day challenge is to help him expand and iron out the world even further.

Challenge Summary and Objectives

I have been working on Pythia for over 8 years.  Over those 8 years, I have thought about a lot and have not always written things down or well enough.  While my storytelling tool kit expands I expand the potential and worldbuilding I can do in Pythia.  Over the 31 days of December, I aim to create small articles about different concepts in my world.  Working to create them in the parts: the Vignette; the core; the meta.

A cool geographic place in your world. What unique or exsotic resourse exsists in your world? Write about a tale surrounding a mythological creature in your world? Write about a political conflict that shaped the political climate of your world.
What criminal organization in your world is notorious and why? What is a villian/person who changed the world's history? Write about a ceremonial building in your world. Describe a lush location in your world.
What are some common insults in your world? Desribe a building that has gone to ruin, and why? Write about how bereal customs work in your world. Write about an important plant.
What is a famous fairy tale? What is a popular sport in the world? What are the rules? Describe an inhospitable area in your world. Describe an isolated settlement; why is it so isolated and where is it?
What is a feared illness? Write about a hero in your world who changed history (none player). Describe a breakthrough magical or technological in your world. Write about an artifact that has shaped history that has no magic to it.
